Experience ESL One Hamburg the best way as a Red Bull VIP

posted by Abelle, 6 days ago
Didn’t get around to buying your tickets for ESL One Hamburg yet? You’re in luck, because joinDOTA and Red Bull have partnered to raffle away a pair of tickets to the best seats in the house.






As a Red Bull VIP you’ll have a spot to watch the games front and center. This means you’ll be close to the action when Team Liquid, Newbee, Virtus.pro and Evil Geniuses take the stage at the first Dota 2 Major of the season!

But that's not all, Red Bull guests will get special treatment. Beside the front row you'll get free food and drinks throughout the event and a support to your travel expenses. Even if you've already booked your tickets to the event this is an upgrade you'll seriously want to consider.

ESL One Hamburg takes place October 28-29 at the Barclaycard Arena in Hamburg, Germany. It features a $1,000,000 prize pool and the top four teams will get a big boost towards qualifying to TI8 with a share of 1500 Qualifying Points. It will be the second Dota 2 Major held in Germany, after The Frankfurt Major in 2015.

To enter, just follow the instructions of our gleam.io raffle. More entries = a better chance to win! The contest ends on Oct 19 at 23:59 CEST, after which we’ll get in touch with the winners.
