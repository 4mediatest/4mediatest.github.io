The Dawn of a New Era - 7.00 hits the main client

posted by guardiangel07, 9 hours ago
The new patch that was released on the test client about a day ago finally hits the main client. This 6.8GB update will change Dota 2 as we know it. Hop in to see what’s new!






A new map
Apart from the new hero, the game itself has many changes. One of the more significant changes is to the map itself.

Roshan has now moved to its own home at near the top Rune spot at the river. In fact, even the runes are different this patch. There are two categories of runes – Powerup Runes and Bounty Runes.

Runes like Double Damage, Arcane, Haste, and Illusion are regarded as Powerup Runes. These runes only spawn on one river location at a time.

Bounty runes now will no longer spawn at the River. There are 4 new rune spots in the jungle that only spawn Bounty Runes every 2 minutes. In fact, if a Bounty Rune is used to replenish the Bottle, it only gives 2 charges instead of 3.


A mini-map view of the new map


The neutral creep camps receive a makeover
Roshan isn’t the only one who decided to pack bags and move. The neutral creeps also have been revamped. Some camps have been moved, some are put to the lower ground, etc.

The Primary jungle is now home to 1 Ancient creep camp, 2 hard camps, 2 medium camps and 1 small camp. The Secondary jungle now has 1 Ancient camp, 1 hard camp, and 1 medium camp.

In fact, there is a new Ancient creep camp that has decided to reside in the lands of Dota 2. It is made up of 1 Prowler Shaman and 2 Prowler Acolytes.


The new Ancient creep


A mini fountain outside the base
The map now has new buildings called Shrines. These are located near the Secret Shops and the new Ancient Camps.

These Shrines when activated provide regeneration in a 500 AoE over 5 seconds. This ability has a 5 minute cooldown period. The Shrines activate by right-clicking it in melee range. Heroes can nonetheless teleport to the Shrines allowing players to quickly reach the jungle.

However, these Shrines can be destroyed. Whenever a team’s Tier 2 towers all fall, the Shrines then becomes vulnerable and can be destroyed providing a 150 gold bounty.

Heroes now carry their own backpacks
Heroes can now carry an additional 3 items in their backpacks. However, items in the backpack cannot be activated nor do they provide passive bonuses.

Nonetheless, items can always be moved between the backpack and main inventory with the item being inactive for 6 seconds when moved from the backpack to the main inventory. However, this penalty does not apply when doing so near a shop.



Hero talents
7.00 also introduces Hero Talents. At levels 10/15/20/25, heroes are offered unique branches of talents that can be acquired. These are usually special bonuses for the hero or the abilities.

Each hero has their own unique set of Talents to choose from. However, choose wisely as bypassed branches are not available at future levels. This means that even if your item build is the same every match, the build still can be different depending on what talents you choose for your hero.



More… more… more!!
The latest patch brings with it so much more updates, from the HUD to how the pregame and even to heroes having a rework. Some heroes have their abilities changed, some are given Aghanim Scepters. There is just so much to learn again on this patch. You can read the different changes here:
